\MakeAbstract{
    本文围绕混合式双轮足机器人的轻量级控制策略开展研究。首先结合机器人产业发展需求，分析双轮足机器人在机械结构设计与运动控制策略方面的国内外研究现状，梳理串联、并联和混合式腿部构型的特点，以及 LQR、VMC、WBC、MPC 和强化学习等典型控制方法的适用场景与局限。随后，面向轻量化、高动态、高刚度和高集成的设计目标，完成整机机械结构方案设计，给出关键性能指标、跳跃系统设计、关节与传动方案以及轮组与执行器选型，为后续控制系统设计和样机实现提供结构基础。
}{双轮足机器人，混合式结构，机械结构设计，轻量级控制}

\MakeAbstractEng{
    This thesis focuses on a lightweight control strategy for a hybrid wheeled-biped robot. It first reviews the domestic and international research status of mechanical configurations and motion control strategies for wheeled-biped robots, including serial, parallel, and hybrid leg structures, as well as representative methods such as LQR, VMC, WBC, MPC, and reinforcement learning. Then, oriented toward lightweight design, high dynamic performance, high stiffness, and high integration, the thesis presents the overall mechanical design, target performance indices, jumping-system configuration, joint and transmission scheme, and actuator selection. These results provide a structural foundation for the subsequent controller design and prototype implementation.
}{wheeled-biped robot, hybrid mechanism, mechanical design, lightweight control}
